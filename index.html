A civic correspondence service honoring the legacy of Martin Luther King Jr. through respectful, values-driven messages to elected officials.

Purpose

This platform enables individuals to send a calm, principled message to a U.S. Senator, encouraging fidelity to democratic norms, moral leadership, and the rule of law.

It is not protest software, not lobbying, and not a fundraising tool. It is a civic expression service designed with operational integrity.
