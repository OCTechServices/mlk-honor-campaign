Transparency

Purpose

The MLK Honor Campaign exists to facilitate respectful civic correspondence aligned with the values of justice, dignity, and nonviolence.

Non-Partisan Commitment

This platform does not endorse political parties, candidates, or legislation. Messages are values-driven, not political directives.

Message Integrity

  • All messages are pre-approved
  • Messages are immutable once queued
  • Each message is cryptographically hashed
  • Status changes are recorded

Financial Transparency

Payments cover operational costs associated with message creation, processing, and respectful delivery.

This is not a donation.

No Guaranteed Outcomes

Delivery of a message does not imply a response or action from any official.
