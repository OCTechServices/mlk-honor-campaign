Transparency

Purpose

The MLK Honor Campaign exists to facilitate respectful civic correspondence aligned with the values of justice, dignity, and nonviolence.

Non-Partisan Commitment

This platform does not endorse political parties, candidates, or legislation. Messages are values-driven, not political directives.

Message Integrity

  • All messages are predefined and publicly visible
  • No custom or user-generated content is accepted
  • Messages are delivered respectfully and without alteration

Payments

  • Payments are processed securely via Stripe
  • Fees support operational costs only
  • This is not a donation platform

Delivery

  • Messages are queued only after successful payment
  • Delivery is not guaranteed to receive a response
  • No follow-up or engagement tracking is performed

Data Use

  • No personal data is sold or shared
  • No tracking pixels or behavioral analytics are used
  • Only operational data required to deliver messages is retained
