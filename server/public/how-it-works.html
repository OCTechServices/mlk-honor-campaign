How It Works

Step-by-Step

1. Select a U.S. Senator.

2. Review a pre-structured, MLK-aligned message.

3. Purchase the message delivery service.

4. Delivery follows U.S. Postal Service conventions used for official correspondence.

  • Messages are prepared for formal, physical delivery.
  • For context, USPS Certified Mail typically cost is often ~$5–$7 with postage.
  • This service covers preparation, handling, tracking, and administrative coordination.
  • If you wish to track your letter, please reach out to the email listed on the face of your receipt. A member will follow up with next steps.

What This Is — and Is Not

This is a paid service for message creation and delivery. It is not a donation, not a protest, and not a lobbying platform.
