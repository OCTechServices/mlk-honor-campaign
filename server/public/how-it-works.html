Four Steps

1
Select a U.S. Senator
Search by state or name. All currently serving senators are available. You choose who receives your message.
2
Review the message
Choose from pre-written, MLK-aligned message templates. Read the full text before proceeding — the content is fixed and delivered exactly as shown. No modifications are accepted.
3
Purchase the delivery service
A flat service fee covers message preparation, envelope addressing, USPS Certified Mail postage, and administrative handling. Payment is processed securely by Stripe.
4
Your letter is prepared and mailed
Upon confirmed payment, your message is queued, printed, and sent via USPS Certified Mail to the senator's official Washington, D.C. office — the address used for formal constituent correspondence. Certified Mail provides a delivery record and requires a signature upon receipt.

Delivery Timeline

From confirmed payment to physical delivery at the senator's office:
